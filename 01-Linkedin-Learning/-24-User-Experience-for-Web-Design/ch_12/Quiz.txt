1- The _____ design principle emphasizes the importance of sites that are easy to learn. It encourages the idea that sites, at their core, should behave like other sites on the web. 

- Answer: standard
You want your site to behave like other sites on the web, but your content should be unique.

2- How will you know if your design decisions have been a success?

- Answer: if users return to your site
This is the mark of a successful website.

3- When making any design decision, what is the most important question to ask yourself?

- Answer: Does the decision decision make the experience better for users?
This is the question at the heart of all user-centric design.
