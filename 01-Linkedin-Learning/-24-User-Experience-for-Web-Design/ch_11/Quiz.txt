1- Your site is heavily reliant on advertisements for revenue. Since you are unable to forego ads entirely, where is the best place to put them on your pages?

- Answer: You should place them in secondary locations or in the margins.
Remember, your content is still the most important thing to visitors, not ads. At the very least, your ads should be consistently located across your site\

2- What are the best types of ads to use on your site?

- Answer: ones that correspond to the site's content
Visitors are usually more accepting of ads if they are relevant to their interests.

3- What is a best practice for using text-based advertisements on your site?

- Answer: Use a consistent format complementary to the rest of the text on your site.
Users should be able to tell the difference between your site's content and advertisements.
