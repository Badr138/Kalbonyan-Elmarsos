1- When writing if/elif chains, the most restrictive conditions should _____.

- Answer: go near the top of the top of the chain

2- Python doesn't natively include a switch structure like Java or C, so how can you emulate switch-like behavior? 

- Answer: Use a dictionary.

3- Why is a dictionary often better to use than a long if/elif chain?

- Answer: A dictionary uses hashing.
