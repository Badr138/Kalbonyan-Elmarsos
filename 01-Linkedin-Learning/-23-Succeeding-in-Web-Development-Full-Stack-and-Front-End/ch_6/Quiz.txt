1- What should you expect to happen at a technical interview?

- Answer: producing code on the spot
Coding on the spot is often intimidating in an interview, so it is best to be prepared.

2- When you change employers, how much of an increase in compensation should you expect?

- Answer: 15 percent
It may not be worth your while to change jobs if you receive any less than this amount.

3- What is the most promising way to find new work?

- Answer: meet-ups
Many hiring authorities look for recommendations from existing employees.

4- You have an attractive offer letter from a new organization but are still a bit reluctant. What should you do?

- Answer: Ask for a few days and examine your current situation.
You might negotiate with your current employer equipped with the offer letter.
