1- Which phrase best exemplifies work as a web developer?

- Answer: continuous learning
You need to continuously adapt to new technology and ideas as they emerge.

2- Your friend declares that they want to work in front-end development for 10 years to make a good salary, and then move on to a more fun career. What should you tell them? 

- Answer: "Unless you really enjoy coding and solving problems, you'll be miserable."
Some people might persevere through 10 years, but they will likely be unsuccessful if they are truly miserable.

3- Your colleague focuses exclusively on graphic design for web pages. What type of web developer are they?

- Answer: front-end
